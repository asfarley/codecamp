Programming 101

An introduction to programming with the C language.

Course Information

Weekend programming classes.
No previous experience required.
Students bring their own computers.
Suitable for ages 14+.
$380 per student.

Location, Time and Dates

Fall 2015: October 31 to December 5
Burnhamthorpe Branch Library
3650 Dixie Rd #101, Mississauga
6 weeks, 2 hours per week on Saturday at 10 AM.

Curriculum

  • basic data types: booleans, integers, strings, floats
  • for-loops, if/else, while, switch statements
  • pointers, memory, arrays
  • functions, headers and libraries
  • source code, compilers, executables
  • debugging
